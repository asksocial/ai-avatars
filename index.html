Features & Functionality

Every Eavesdrop avatar ships with the same conversational core, then dials in the policies, integrations, and tone for the brand it represents.

i

Voice & text in one

Visitors can speak or type. The avatar replies in either modality, with natural lip-sync and a brand-matched voice profile.

ii

Trained on your content

We pull from your website, knowledge base, and approved documents. The avatar answers from your sources, not the open internet.

iii

Smart routing

Questions outside the avatar's scope (billing, clinical, escalation) get routed back to a real human on your team, with full transcript context.

iv

Compliance built-in

Educational disclaimers, PHI guardrails, and refusal policies are wired in by default and reviewed for every regulated vertical.

v

Drop-in embed

One snippet on your site, hosted on your subdomain, with no plugin or rebuild required. Updates push instantly from the Eavesdrop Portal.

vi

Analytics & transcripts

Every conversation is logged, summarized, and tagged by intent, so you can see what visitors actually ask, week over week.

Our Secret Sauce

Anyone can use a knowledge base to train an AI to respond in a compliant manner. But through our social-listening expertise, we mine posts, comments, community threads, and caregiver discussions for the exact points of confusion people hit along their journey.

By leveraging real conversations around uncertainty and pairing them with your verified content, we build frameworks that recognize risk and understand natural language — so an avatar can respond with empathy and authority, in real time.

  • 1 Listen — mine real conversations for where confusion and risk actually live.
  • 2 Frame — pair those signals with your content into risk-aware language models.
  • 3 Deploy — a live avatar that answers with empathy and escalates safely.
